What We Do

We are able to create marker-based, marker-less, and location-based augmented reality experiences.

Marker-based

Pointing a camera at a specific trigger point (AR marker), the user can see and interact with life-like digital overlays and multimedia content.

Markerless

3D content overlaid right onto the user's surroundings. The most sophisticated form of augmented reality, markerless AR is based on the simultaneous localization and mapping (SLAM) technology.

Location-based

With aid of GPS, entering a particular location will trigger augmented 2D/3D animation, sound or video.

Empowerment through story telling

Problem

  • Lack of a dedicated platform for the indigenous people to present and sell their authentic handcrafts and other traditional products.
  • Untapped potential of Augmented Reality for the promotion of the indigenous culture, tradition, ancient knowledge, language, and art the way they deserve.

Solution

An online AR-powered C2C marketplace dedicated to indigenous people, allowing them to sell their different handicrafts directly to their enthusiasts while promoting their culture and history.
